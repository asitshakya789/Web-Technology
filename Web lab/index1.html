Computer Science Department

The Computer Science Department offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers the principles of computing and programming. Our faculty members are experts in their fields and are dedicated to providing quality education.

Electrical Engineering Department

The Electrical Engineering Department focuses on the study of electrical systems, electronics, and electromagnetism. Students gain hands-on experience through labs and projects.

Mechanical Engineering Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department provides education in the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. Our programs emphasize practical skills and innovative thinking.

Civil Engineering Department

The Civil Engineering Department trains students in the planning, design, and construction of infrastructure projects. Our curriculum includes both theoretical and practical components.
